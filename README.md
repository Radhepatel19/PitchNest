🚀 PitchNest – Business Idea Sharing Platform

PitchNest is a mobile application designed for students, entrepreneurs, and innovators to discover and share business ideas. It provides a simple and organized platform where users can explore trending ideas and showcase their own concepts.

🌟 Features
💡 Explore Business Ideas
Browse trending and categorized business ideas shared by users.
✍️ Post Your Own Ideas
Users can add and describe their business ideas with images and details.
🔍 Search & Categories
Easily find ideas using search and category filters.
📈 Trending Ideas Section
Discover popular ideas based on user engagement.
👤 User Profiles
Manage your ideas and personal profile.
🧑‍💻 Tech Stack
Frontend: Android Studio (Java/Kotlin) / Flutter
Backend: Firebase, Gemini API
Database: Firebase (Firestore / Realtime Database)
Cloud: Cloudinary / Cloud Storage
🎯 Use Cases
Students looking for project or startup ideas
Entrepreneurs exploring new concepts
Users sharing innovative thoughts
